How to Play
›
1
Connect tiles with the same value by dragging your finger across them.
2
After linking two equal tiles, you can chain the next double — e.g. 2→2→4→8→16.
3
You can also repeat the last value before doubling: 2→2→2→4→8.
4
When you lift your finger, all selected tiles merge into one at the last position.
5
Reach the GOAL block shown on the progress bar to complete the level!
Help / Info
›
∞
The journey is infinite — every phase doubles the GOAL block. Complete a phase to unlock the next one on the map.
▮
The progress bar starts each phase at 50% and fills in real time as you build toward the GOAL block.
💎
Earn diamonds from big merges (128+), level-up rewards, daily rewards and the shop. Spend them on powerups.
💾
Your board, level and diamonds are saved automatically — close the game and continue anytime.
Powerups Info
›
⇄
Swap (💎75): pick two blocks and they trade places. Perfect for lining up a big chain.
🔨
Smash (💎75): the hammer destroys one block — clear junk that's blocking your build.
x2
Double Up: when a phase gets stuck, an offer appears to double your 2nd biggest block — pay 💎75 or watch an ad for free.
